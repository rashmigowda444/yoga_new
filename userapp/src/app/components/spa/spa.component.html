SPA SERVICES



DEFINITION :

Spa believes that the elimination of stress and tension is critical for the improvement and maintenance of overall well being. Spa works to improve quality of life through physical and mental rejuvenation. Self esteem and a balance of health and wellness are achieved in the Spa.


BRIEF DESCRIPTION :

Spa Treatments, designed to satisfy the whole body, are provided by highly trained staff in the field of massage therapy, esthetics and skincare as well as a full range of body therapies, group and corporate clientele with hands-on professional service.
Spa therapies have existed since the classical times when taking bath with water was considered as a popular means to treat illnesses.
