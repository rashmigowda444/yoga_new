HISTORY OF YOGA



Yoga originated in ancient India thousands of years ago. The word “yoga” derives from the Sanskrit (ancient Indian language) word “yug,” meaning to yoke, or unite . Very systematic processes of yoga were written by great Sage Patanjali in 500BC. His path of Ashtanga yoga included yamas and niyamas, Asanas, Pranayama, Pratyahara, Dharana, Dhyan, Samadhi.

Though many people in modern times think of yoga as just a physical practice that can help you become healthier physically, the deeper purpose of yoga practice is deeply spiritual and intended to help unite your individual soul with the universal consciousness.
